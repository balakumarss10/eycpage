A skill-based curriculum for a great start to learning

The early years of a child's education are vital for developing their critical thinking and problem-solving skills. We at 21K School - EYLC have created an environment in which our young students feel empowered to ask questions, safe to meet challenges head on, and accompanied on their journey towards reaching their full potential.

The Early Years Curriculum provides an age-appropriate, activity-based academic plan for every grade. The curriculum content design is based on the U.K.'s Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework that is globally recognized and accepted, alongside best educational practices with an emphasis on every child’s unique learning styles. Values, and ethics for the preschoolers are weaved into our programs with stories & narratives using the "Principles of 16 Habits of Mind", (by Art Costa and Bena Kallick) to nurture & build a strong character, independent mind and confident leaders of tomorrow.

The main subject areas are:

  • Literacy
  • Numeracy
  • Understanding the world
  • Expressive Arts & Designs
  • Communication & Language Development
  • Physical Development
  • Personal, Social and Emotional Development

At 21K School, we believe that a bright start to young learner’s educational journey is the key to lifelong learning.

Our Early Years Learning Centre (EYLC) provides them with the perfect start, empowering them with skills, confidence, and understanding that will take them through their years of learning.

We provide an enriching learning and physical campus environment, designed to give your child an effective foundation to grow. With our talented professionals and experienced faculty, each student's potential can be explored and nurtured within our safe and inspiring international programme.

As a part of 21K School, Asia's largest online school with 7500+ students from 78 different countries, we give our little learners the perfect opportunity to explore new aspects of education which are not limited by physical boundaries or classroom walls.

Come join us, and let us pave the way for your child's future!
